- STRICT (default for merge-gate reviews)

   - Severity: [P0|P1|P2]

4. Keep each comment <= 6 lines.
5. Output in English only.
6. Do not invent facts beyond the shown diff and PR description.

- Final decision: Approve | Request changes | Comment
